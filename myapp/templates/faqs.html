Getting Started

What is ContractorHub and how does it work?

ContractorHub is Pakistan's leading platform connecting skilled workers (electricians, plumbers, painters, carpenters, etc.) with clients who need their services.

  • Clients post jobs or browse worker profiles
  • Workers apply for jobs or get direct hire requests
  • Both parties agree on terms and price
  • Work is completed, payment is released through our secure system
  • Both parties leave reviews for each other

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is free and takes less than 2 minutes:

  • Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
  • Choose whether you're a Client or a Worker
  • Enter your name, email, and phone number
  • Set a strong password
  • Verify your email address
Tip: You can also sign up quickly using your Google or Facebook account.

Is ContractorHub free to use?

Signing up and browsing ContractorHub is completely free. We only charge a small service fee when a job is successfully completed:

  • Workers: 10% service fee on earnings
  • Clients: 5% processing fee on job cost

There are no hidden fees, subscription charges, or listing fees.

Can I use ContractorHub in my city?

ContractorHub currently operates in 30+ cities across Pakistan including:

  • Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi
  • Faisalabad, Multan, Gujranwala, Sialkot
  • Peshawar, Quetta, Hyderabad, and many more

We are rapidly expanding. If your city isn't listed, sign up anyway — we'll notify you when we launch near you!

Can I be both a client and a worker?

Yes! One account can be switched between Client mode and Worker mode using the "Switch Mode" option in your profile dropdown. This is perfect for people who both hire others and offer their own services.

How do I find workers near me?

Finding workers is easy:

  • Go to the Client Dashboard
  • Use the search bar to search by skill (e.g., "plumber")
  • Filter by location, rating, price, or availability
  • Browse profiles and view ratings and reviews
  • Click "Hire" to send a job request

Payments & Fees

How does payment work on ContractorHub?

We use a secure escrow system to protect both clients and workers:

  • Client pays when accepting a job proposal
  • Money is held securely in escrow by ContractorHub
  • Worker completes the job
  • Client confirms completion
  • Payment is released to the worker within 3-5 business days
Protection: Your money is safe until you confirm the job is done to your satisfaction.

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept the following payment methods:

  • JazzCash
  • EasyPaisa
  • Bank Transfer (all major Pakistani banks)
  • Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)

We are working on adding more payment options soon.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are available in the following cases:

  • Worker cancels the job before starting
  • Worker does not show up without notice
  • Work quality is significantly below agreed standards (after dispute review)

To request a refund, go to My Jobs → Dispute → Submit Claim within 7 days of job completion. Our team will review within 5 business days.

When do workers receive their payment?

After the client confirms job completion, payment is released within 3-5 business days to your registered payment method. Workers can track their pending and received payments in the Earnings section of their dashboard.

Why can't I pay the worker directly?

Off-platform payments are prohibited because:

  • You lose payment protection if something goes wrong
  • Workers have no guarantee of getting paid
  • Disputes cannot be resolved by our team
  • It violates our Terms of Service and may result in account suspension

Our escrow system exists to protect everyone involved.

For Workers

How do I create a worker profile?

After signing up, switch to Worker Mode from your profile. Then:

  • Add your skills and experience
  • Set your hourly rate or daily rate
  • Add your service area / city
  • Upload a profile photo
  • Complete CNIC verification for the "Verified" badge
Tip: Profiles with photos and CNIC verification get 3x more job offers.

How do I get verified on ContractorHub?

Verification requires:

  • Valid CNIC (both sides photo)
  • A selfie with your CNIC
  • Phone number verification (OTP)

Verification is reviewed within 24 hours. Verified workers receive a blue checkmark and appear higher in search results.

How do I apply for jobs?

There are two ways to get work on ContractorHub:

  • Active: Browse the job feed and apply with your proposal and price
  • Passive: Clients find your profile and send you direct hire requests

A complete profile means more clients will find and contact you directly.

What happens if a client doesn't pay?

All payments go through our escrow system — this means the client's payment is already secured before you start work. You will always get paid for completed work done through ContractorHub's official system. Never start work without an accepted job on the platform.

How can I improve my rating?

  • Always arrive on time and communicate proactively
  • Complete work to the agreed standard
  • Be polite and professional at all times
  • Ask satisfied clients to leave a review
  • Respond to messages quickly

Can I set my own rates?

Yes! You have full control over your rates. You can set an hourly rate on your profile, and you can also negotiate custom rates for specific jobs. The final price is always agreed between you and the client before work begins.

For Clients

How do I hire a worker?

  • Search for the skill you need (e.g., "electrician Lahore")
  • Browse profiles, check ratings and reviews
  • Click "Hire" on the worker's profile
  • Describe your job and agree on price
  • Make payment to secure the booking
  • Worker arrives and completes the job
  • Confirm completion to release payment

How do I know workers are trustworthy?

We take trust very seriously. All workers on ContractorHub:

  • Are verified with CNIC before getting the "Verified" badge
  • Have ratings and reviews from past clients
  • Are subject to our Code of Conduct
  • Can be reported and banned for misconduct
Always hire verified workers for maximum peace of mind.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

If you're not satisfied:

  • First, communicate with the worker and give them a chance to fix the issue
  • If unresolved, raise a dispute through My Jobs → Dispute within 7 days
  • Our team will review evidence from both sides
  • A resolution (refund, partial refund, or release of payment) will be provided

Do not confirm job completion until you are satisfied with the work.

How do I post a job?

  • Go to Client Dashboard → Post a Job
  • Select the skill category
  • Describe the job in detail (location, scope, timeline)
  • Set your budget
  • Workers in your area will apply with proposals
  • Review proposals and hire the best fit

Can I hire the same worker again?

Absolutely! You can save workers to your "Favorites" list and hire them directly for future jobs. Repeat hires do not go through the search process — just visit their profile and click Hire again.

Safety & Trust

How does ContractorHub verify workers?

Our verification process includes:

  • CNIC verification (photo ID check)
  • Phone number OTP verification
  • Selfie with ID match
  • Optional: skill test or certification upload

Workers who pass all checks receive a verified badge on their profile.

How do I report a problem or suspicious user?

You can report any user directly from their profile by clicking the "Report" button. You can also contact our support team at safety@contractorhub.pk. We take all reports seriously and investigate within 24 hours.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes. We use SSL encryption for all data, store sensitive information encrypted, and never sell your data to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for details on how we protect your information.

What should I do if I feel unsafe?

Your safety comes first. If you ever feel unsafe:

  • Leave the situation immediately
  • Contact emergency services if needed (Police: 15)
  • Report the incident to ContractorHub at safety@contractorhub.pk
  • We will take immediate action on your report

Account & Settings

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email. You'll receive a reset link within 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

How do I change my email or phone number?

Go to Account Settings → Personal Information. You can update your email or phone number. A verification code will be sent to confirm the change. For security, you'll need to verify both your old and new contact information.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove your profile, reviews, and history. Any pending payments will be processed before deletion. The process takes up to 30 days.

How do I turn off email notifications?

Go to Account Settings → Notifications. You can customize which notifications you receive via email, SMS, or in-app. You can turn off marketing emails while keeping important job and payment alerts.

My account is suspended — what do I do?

If your account is suspended, you will receive an email explaining the reason. You can appeal by emailing support@contractorhub.pk with your account details and explanation. Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.
