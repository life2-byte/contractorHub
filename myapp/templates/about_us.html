Who We Are

More Than Just a Platform

We started in 2025 with a mission to solve Pakistan's biggest challenge in the labor market — trust. Finding a reliable worker or a fair client was always hit or miss. ContractorHub changes that.

We combine technology with community to ensure every transaction is safe, transparent, and beneficial for both sides. From electricians and plumbers to farm workers and drivers — we cover it all.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small idea to Pakistan's fastest growing contractor platform — here's our story.

The Idea is Born

Four university friends noticed how hard it was to find reliable workers for home renovations. ContractorHub was sketched on a whiteboard in a hostel room.

2024
Jan '25

Beta Launch in Lahore

We launched our beta with 200 verified workers in Lahore. Within 2 weeks, 500 clients had signed up and our first 100 jobs were completed.

Expanded to 5 Cities

After overwhelming success in Lahore, we expanded to Karachi, Islamabad, Faisalabad, and Multan — bringing 10,000+ workers onto the platform.

Mid '25
2026

50,000 Workers & Growing

Today, ContractorHub connects over 50,000 verified workers with 12,000+ clients across 30 cities. And we're just getting started.
