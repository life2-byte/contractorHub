Introduction

Last updated: Jan 1, 2026

Welcome to ContractorHub. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform.

Important: By using ContractorHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

ContractorHub operates as a marketplace connecting clients with skilled workers across Pakistan. We take our responsibility to protect your data very seriously.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and phone number during registration
  • Profile information including skills, experience, and location
  • Payment information (processed securely, not stored by us)
  • Messages and communications through our platform
  • Reviews and ratings you submit

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited and time spent on platform
  • Search queries and job browsing history
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Information from Third Parties

If you sign in via Google or Facebook, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) as permitted by your privacy settings on those platforms.

How We Use Your Information

To Provide Our Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match clients with suitable workers
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Facilitate communication between users

To Improve ContractorHub

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Conduct research and development
  • Monitor and fix technical issues
  • Personalize your experience

For Safety & Security

  • Verify worker and client identities
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We May Share With

  • Other Users: Your profile information is visible to relevant clients or workers as needed for the service
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics tools (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice to users)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Strict access controls — only authorized staff can access user data
  • Two-factor authentication available for accounts

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In case of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours.

Cookies Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on ContractorHub.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function (login sessions, security)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for relevant advertising (can be disabled)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@contractorhub.pk

Eligibility & Accounts

Who Can Use ContractorHub

  • You must be at least 18 years of age
  • You must be a resident of Pakistan or authorized to work in Pakistan
  • You must provide accurate and complete registration information
  • One account per person — multiple accounts are not permitted

Account Responsibilities

  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password
  • You are responsible for all activities that occur under your account
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized use of your account
  • Do not share your account credentials with others

Platform Rules

For All Users

  • Be honest and accurate in all communications and profiles
  • Treat other users with respect and professionalism
  • Do not post false, misleading, or fraudulent information
  • Do not harass, threaten, or abuse other users
  • Do not circumvent our platform to avoid fees

For Workers

  • Only apply for jobs within your genuine skill set
  • Complete jobs as agreed — abandoning jobs without notice is a violation
  • Maintain professional conduct at all job sites
  • Respond to client messages within 24 hours

For Clients

  • Provide accurate job descriptions and requirements
  • Pay workers fairly and on time as agreed
  • Do not request work outside the agreed scope without additional compensation
  • Leave honest and fair reviews after job completion

Payments & Fees

Platform Fees

  • ContractorHub charges a service fee on completed transactions
  • Workers: 10% service fee deducted from earnings
  • Clients: 5% processing fee added to job cost
  • Fees are clearly shown before any transaction is confirmed

Payment Terms

  • All payments must be processed through ContractorHub's secure payment system
  • Payments are held in escrow until job completion is confirmed
  • Worker payouts are processed within 3-5 business days after job completion
  • Refunds are subject to our dispute resolution process

Off-platform payments are prohibited. Any attempt to arrange payments outside ContractorHub to avoid fees may result in account suspension.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on ContractorHub:

  • Creating fake profiles or impersonating others
  • Posting spam, scam listings, or fraudulent job offers
  • Using the platform for illegal activities of any kind
  • Scraping, copying, or reproducing platform content without permission
  • Attempting to hack, exploit, or damage the platform
  • Discriminating against users based on religion, gender, ethnicity, or disability
  • Manipulating ratings or reviews through fake submissions
  • Sharing other users' personal information without consent

Violations may result in immediate account suspension and legal action where applicable.

Dispute Resolution

Dispute Process

  • Either party can raise a dispute within 7 days of job completion
  • ContractorHub's dispute team will review evidence from both sides
  • A resolution will be provided within 5 business days
  • ContractorHub's decision is final for disputes under Rs 50,000

Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of Pakistan. Any legal disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan.

Termination by ContractorHub
